# 🌱 EcoSort AI

### Smart Waste Segregation & Policy RAG Assistant

EcoSort AI is an AI-powered sustainability platform designed to improve waste segregation practices through interactive waste classification, municipal policy awareness, and environmental impact tracking.

The project combines **computer vision concepts**, **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)**, and an intuitive user experience to educate citizens about responsible waste disposal while promoting the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 11 & SDG 12).

---

## 🚀 Features

### ♻️ AI Waste Scanner

* Scan common household waste items.
* Upload waste images for classification.
* Live camera support for real-time waste identification.
* Displays disposal instructions and environmental warnings.

### 🧠 Policy RAG Assistant

* Simulates Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).
* Retrieves relevant municipal waste management policies.
* Provides context-aware responses based on government guidelines.
* Demonstrates transparent AI reasoning through retrieval traces.

### 📊 Sustainability Dashboard

* Tracks total segregated items.
* Calculates estimated CO₂ offset.
* Monitors recycling rates.
* Awards EcoScore points to encourage responsible behavior.

### 🔍 RAG Pipeline Inspector

* Displays retrieved document chunks.
* Shows prompt construction process.
* Promotes Responsible AI and explainability.

## 📸 Usage

### Waste Scanner

1. Navigate to **AI Waste Scanner**.
2. Choose one of the following modes:

   * Sample Items
   * Upload Image
   * Live Camera
3. Review the generated segregation report.
4. Log the action to update sustainability metrics.

### Policy Assistant

1. Open the **Policy RAG Chat** tab.
2. Ask questions such as:

   * *How should I dispose of batteries?*
   * *What are the fines for mixing wet and dry waste?*
   * *Are greasy pizza boxes recyclable?*
3. View retrieved policy chunks and generated responses.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Improper waste segregation contributes to:

* Increased landfill contamination.
* Lower recycling efficiency.
* Public unawareness of municipal waste regulations.
* Higher environmental and economic costs.

EcoSort AI addresses these challenges by making sustainability education interactive, accessible, and AI-driven.
